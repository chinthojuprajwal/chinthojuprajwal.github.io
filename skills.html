Music and Guitar Playing

I'm a huge guitar geek and this started as a way to make productive use of my time during quarantine. I listen to a lot of Metal and Rock Albums, yet I don't shy away from any other genres. I also appreciate exotic music such as Persian and Hindustani. My favorite bands that I listen to every day are Metallica and Megadeth. I love playing every genre from James Hetfield's clean rhythm parts to Marty Friedman's solos to George Harrison's melodies. I own a Squire Strat and a Fender 10G Amp. I swapped out and replaced the guitar with better strings and a pickup selector. Music has been a great companion for me in every mood and I used music as a reward mechanism for being productive. Check out some of my covers on my Insta.
